OUR SERVICES

  1. Booking Platform: A user-friendly website where customers can browse through various vacation tour packages offered by JE, select their preferred destinations, accommodation options, activities, and book their trips online.


  2. Interactive Tour Guides: An online platform or mobile app providing interactive tour guides for popular vacation spots. These guides could include detailed information about attractions, restaurants, local customs, and insider tips.


  3. Customized Trip Planning: A service where customers can input their preferences, budget, and desired destinations, and JE's team of travel experts creates personalized vacation itineraries for them.


  4. Virtual Tours: Immersive virtual reality or 360-degree tours of popular vacation destinations, allowing customers to explore and experience different locations from the comfort of their own homes.


  5. Travel Blog and Reviews: A travel blog on JE's website featuring articles, travel guides, and reviews written by experienced travelers and local experts. This can help customers gather information and make informed decisions about their vacation plans.


  6. Online Customer Support: Live chat support or a customer service hotline where customers can get assistance with booking inquiries, itinerary changes, or any other questions they may have before, during, or after their trip.


  7. Mobile App: A mobile application that allows customers to access their booking information, receive real-time updates on their trip, and access exclusive deals and discounts for future vacations.


  8. Social Media Integration: Integration with social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter, where customers can share their travel experiences, photos, and reviews, and JE can engage with its audience and showcase its services.


  9. Travel Insurance: Option to purchase travel insurance directly through JE's website to protect customers against unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.


  10. Group Travel Management: A feature for organizing and managing group travel bookings, allowing group leaders to easily coordinate with JE and keep track of all trip details for their members.
